# Privacy Policy for Kwots

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

## Overview

Kwots ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal quote collection app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Storage

All quotes and preferences you create in Kwots are stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your quote collection is never uploaded to our servers. We do not have access to your quotes.

## AI Features

When you use AI-powered features (such as Discover, AI's Take, Morning Brew, Find More, or text-to-speech), the text of relevant quotes is sent to the following third-party services to generate responses:

- **Google Generative AI (Gemini)** for quote suggestions, reflections, stories, and insights
- **Google Cloud Text-to-Speech** for reading AI content aloud

These requests are made over encrypted HTTPS connections. Only the quote text necessary to fulfill your request is sent. No personal information, device identifiers, or account data is included.

When Google services are unavailable, the app may use **Apple Intelligence** (on-device processing) as a fallback. In this case, no data leaves your device.

For more information about how Google handles data:
- Google AI Privacy: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
- Google Cloud Privacy: https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice

## Camera, Microphone, and Photos

Kwots may request access to your camera, microphone, photo library, and speech recognition. These are used **exclusively** for capturing quotes:

- **Camera**: To photograph text for OCR (optical character recognition)
- **Photo Library**: To extract text from existing images
- **Microphone & Speech Recognition**: To dictate quotes by voice

All processing is performed on your device. No images, audio, or recordings are sent to external servers.

## No Personal Information

Kwots does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no email collection, no login, and no registration.

## No Analytics or Tracking

Kwots does not use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect usage data or behavioral information.

## Notifications

Kwots may send you local push notifications (such as Morning Brew reminders) if you enable them. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external server.

## Children's Privacy

Kwots does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal information from any user, it is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
